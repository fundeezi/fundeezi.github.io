How To Sell

Fundeezi is truly a hands-free, cost-free fundraiser. I'm sure your organziation is no stranger to fundraising. Fundeezi has tried to make the process as streamlined as possible so you can focus on what you do.

The app is purchased directly through the app store and none of your members have to keep up with cards, cookies, or overpriced novelty items. You simply spread the word about your campaign to supporters and donors and wait for the check to come in.

Tips for Successful Fundraiser

Stir up excitement about your campaign by notifying all of your members and supporters about Fundeezi.

You already have social media experts, take advantage of them. Your organization's members have all of the tools they need to build a successful campaign.

Offer prizes or awards to the top selling members. Teams love competition and it will bring success to your campaign.

Commit to a number. Having a goal allows your members to achieve higher goals.
