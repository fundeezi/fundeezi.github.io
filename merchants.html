Merchants

Fundeezi is a mobile application that features coupons and savings available through local fundraising.

  • Submit a coupon or savings for a set amount of time

    When you choose to partner with Fundeezi and support local organizations you will fill out our merchant agreement form. Once all details are finalized your coupon or discount will be uploaded into the Fundeezi app for the amount of time that you choose. Organizations in your local area will be able to create a campaign and sell the Fundeezi app and financially benefit from purchases.

  • Customer presents app with coupon at checkout

    Once the customer has purchased the app they will now have access to coupons included. Your business’s coupon will be presented to cashier, server or responsible party. Restrictions on the coupon or discount will be clearly marked on the coupon and agreed to by the purchaser of the Fundeezi app.

  • Security measures are checked by responsible party

    Fundeezi has multiple security measures that help your business secure the legitimacy of the coupon or discount. A logo will continuously spin at the bottom of your coupon to ensure that a screenshot is not being used. The ability of the responsible party to make customer open the app in front of them can ensure that the app is real and working correctly.

  • Customer saves money while helping local organizations

    Fundraisers are a great way for organizations to raise money needed for operation. From sports teams to church groups, your coupons and discounts will help these organizations raise money in the most efficient manner. Your partnership with Fundeezi shows that your business supports the community in a proactive way and customers will be able to purchase a product that will pay for itself.

  • The community will promote your business

    A partnership with Fundeezi is a partnership with your community. Customers will know that your business is giving back to help local organizations. Members of each organization will be promoting your discounts and coupons bringing new customers in your door. Customers and organizations will be able to share with their social media network about the visit to your business.

Merchants FAQ’s

What is Fundeezi?

Fundeezi is a mobile application that offers coupons and savings through local fundraising organizations. The discounts you provide will directly help these organizations in your community. Our hope is to build a longterm partnership with you to help local organizations raise the funds needed for their operation.

How does offering a coupon or discount to Fundeezi help my business?

Providing discounts for local organizations to sell helps your business by reaching new customers. Not only does it show that you support local organizations and the community, it will also bring new customers in that are taking advantage of the discounts.

How much does it cost to partner with Fundeezi?

The cost to you is absolutely free. Unlike many coupon distribution options, Fundeezi distributes your coupons to future customers for free. Organizations will be working hard to expand your customer base and let others know that your business is supporting the community.

What is the normal discount?

It varies for each merchant. The better the deal, the more enticing it will be for app buyers to visit your business. We do ask that you provide us with a discount that can be committed to for the amount of time specified by you.

How long will the discount be offered?

We like to offer discounts for 6 months to 1 year. This allows the customer to visit your business multiple times and hopefully gain a longterm customer.

Will Fundeezi advertise for your business?

With your permission we would like to partner in advertising that would benefit everyone. Social media is a great platform that Fundeezi takes advantage of. We would love to promote your deal or discount via social media to offer the greatest exposure for both of us.

What are the rules on the coupon or discount?

Disclaimer on Coupon: Coupons are nontransferable. This offer is not valid in combination with any other offers, discounts or coupons. Present the app when ordering, equal or lesser value always applies. Tax and gratuity not included. This coupon is valid only at participating locations. Limit one coupon per party per visit.

What are the terms between Fundeezi and my business?

The only commitment is that the merchant is obligated to honor the coupon between the start date and end date, which they have agreed to place with Fundeezi. Merchant also allows use of any logos/other materials to represent their business. We also ask that you provide us with any bar codes that you offer in order for it to be easy to redeem for your employees.

What kind of security measures will there be in the app?

To prevent a screenshot on a smart phone, your coupon will include a spinning Fundeezi logo. This will prevent a user from transferring a picture from their app to another phone. We also ask that you have the customer exit the coupon and reopen it to ensure legitimacy.

How do we get started?

All that is required is fill out our Merchant Service Agreement. It requires contact information; discount details, agreement and signature.
