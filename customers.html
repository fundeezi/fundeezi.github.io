Customers FAQ’s

What is Fundeezi?

Fundeezi is a mobile application that offers coupons and savings through local fundraising organizations. Fundeezi seeks discounts from local merchants to benefit organizations. We hope to offer you great savings and discounts that will directly help these local organizations.

How do I use the app?

After purchasing the app from the respective app store, you will be prompted for your zip code and then provided a list of all of the organizations in that area that are currently running a campaign. Select the group that you want to benefit from your purchase and then select the code of the person who told you about it. You will then have access to multiple discounts for 1 year from purchase. The coupons will have an expiration date. Coupons will be added throughout the year.

How long do I have access to the app?

You will have access to the app for 1 year from purchase. You will be notified and given the option to repurchase the app and support the same organization or a different one.

How many times can I use the app?

Most of our coupons are 1 use per day unless specified otherwise.

Will the app work at all of the locations?

Unless specified otherwise, the app is able to be used only at the address listed on the coupon.

Are there any restrictions?

Disclaimer on Coupon: Coupons are nontransferable. This offer is not valid in combination with any other offers, discounts or coupons. Present the app when ordering, equal or lesser value always applies. Tax and gratuity not included. This coupon is valid only at participating locations. Limit one coupon per party per visit.

My app is not working, who do I contact?

Please notify us immediately at support@fundeezi.com
