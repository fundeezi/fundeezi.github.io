Fundraisers FAQ's

What is Fundeezi?

Fundeezi is a mobile application that offers coupons and savings through local fundraising organizations. Fundeezi seeks discounts from local merchants to benefit your organization as well as your supporters. Our hope is to build a longterm partnership with you to help you raise the funds needed for your organization's continued success.

How does the app work?

Once your organization agrees to the terms and fills out the organization agreement, you will be put in the system and purchasers of the app can select your organization as the beneficiary. The customer will then be able to access all of the deals and coupons until it expires.

How long will my customers get to keep the app?

The app will be active for one year and then at the end of that year the app will no longer be usable and the customer will be prompted to repurchase the app for another year. As long as the customer selects your organization as the beneficiary you will receive the proceeds of that purchase.

Are there any upfront costs for my organization?

There are no upfront costs to selling the app. Fundeezi takes care of securing the businesses that are included in the app, and the costs of the operation of the app.

What is the cost of the app?

The app is $20 and can pay for itself in a matter of days.

How do we sign up?

You will need to fill out the organization agreement, once Fundeezi looks over your information you will move into the approval process.

Can any organization sign up to sell the app?

Yes any organization can sell the mobile app. Fundeezi does not deal with any organizations that offer or are involved in:
  • Adult services or products
  • Sexually explicit material
  • Pornography of any kind
  • Underage consumption of alcohol, tobacco, or narcotics
  • Drug use or paraphernalia
  • Funding for abortion
  • Hurtful or hateful language
  • Violent or hateful material
  • Bigotry, racism, sexism, or profanity
  • False or misleading statements
  • Gangs or organized violence
  • Hate groups or terrorist organizations

This does not cover every scenario and Fundeezi will review organizations on a case by case basis.

Who secures the businesses for the app?

Fundeezi works hard to get the best businesses in a commuity to join in helping local organizations. We will secure all of the businesses that are offering discounts or coupons.

Can we offer our own business to add to the app?

You can, however the businesses deals will not be unique to only your organization. A merchant agrees to provide discounts to all of the organizations that Fundeezi approves.

What type of merchants are on the app?

We go after deals from local businesses. These are the small businesses that want to help out local organizations and gain new business through your supporters and donors.

How much of the sales price does our organization get to keep?

Website maintenance, app maintenance and sales will account for 40% of the proceeds from the sale of the app. Your organization will get 60% of the proceeds.

Can the card be used in any location?

If a deal is specific to one location or multiple locations it will be noted on the coupon itself.

Can we track which person in our organization sold the app?

Yes, each person in your organization will be assigned a code and the administrator will be able to check on everyone's progress.

What is the best way to sell the app?

Please go to our website and click on “how do we sell”. There are many different avenues to sell the app. The ease of reaching out to supoorters of your organization on social media make for a quick burst of sales. Word of mouth and setting up a booth, or free carwash with purchase of the app are other ideas that could gain traction.
