About the Fundeezi App

Fundeezi is a mobile application that features coupons and savings available through local fundraising organizations.

Fundeezi seeks discounts from local merchants to benefit your organization as well as your supporters.

Our hope is to build a long-term partnernship with your organization and help you raise the funds needed for continued success.

Get Started

Benefits Fundeezi App

  • No Upfront Costs

    Organizations are able to partner with Fundeezi for free. There are no hidden costs.

  • Earn a 60% commission

    Your organization will keep 60% of the cost of the app. This allows you to retain a large profit through your member sales.

  • Easily share on social media

    Social media is a great way to spread the word about your campaign. Everyone benefits - from customer to merchant.

  • Quick setup process

    Once we approve your campaign and create your account, your members will be able to start offering the app to potential customers immediately.

  • Create a campaign

    Whether you need to raise a large amout of money quickly or small amounts of money over a longer period of time, Fundeezi is here to help. Creating a campaign is as easy as filling out our organizational agreement and spreading the word with your memebers and supporters.

  • Share the campaign with donors

    Engaging supporters and donors via social media is a free and effective tool to sell the Fundeezi app. The benefit of our app to your supporters makes selling a win-win-win. Your supporters, your organization, and the local merchants will all benefit from your campaign.

  • Get paid

    Fundeezi has made it easy for your organization to reap the benefits of a campaign. All transactions are made through the approriate app store and Fundeezi keeps you updated on your organization's progress. Your organization will receive a check for 60% of the profit. It's that easy!
