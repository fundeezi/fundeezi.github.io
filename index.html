About Fundeezi App

Fundeezi is a mobile application that features coupons and savings available through local fundraising.

Our goal is to help underfunded organizations raise money through Fundeezi. Discounts will be directly affecting the local organizations that choose to sell the Fundeezi app.

We hope to from a partnership with you and help you raise necessary funds for your organization.
